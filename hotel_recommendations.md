# Hotel Recommendations — Naples / Sorrento / Salerno (17–24 Oct 2026)

**Brief:** couple (~19), private double, "cheap but not dogshit", target **€50–80/night for two**. October = shoulder season — prices drop vs summer but Sorrento stays the expensive base. Book 2–4 months out with **free cancellation on Booking.com**, then re-check prices a month before.

**Price reality per base (Oct, private double, decent reviews):**
- **Naples: €50–75** — easily inside budget, best value of the trip.
- **Sorrento: €75–110** — the budget-buster; €80ish gets decent, under €70 gets sketchy. Consider Sant'Agnello (1 Circumvesuviana stop) to save.
- **Salerno: €45–70** — cheapest base, quality high. This subsidizes Sorrento.
- 7-night blended average lands ≈ €65–80/night — on target.

---

## NAPLES — 3 nights (17–20 Oct)

### Where to stay
- ✅ **Centro Storico / Decumani (Spaccanapoli, Via dei Tribunali, Piazza Bellini)** — the correct answer for a first visit: safe-by-crowds, walkable to everything, best pizza, 10 min walk or 1 metro stop from Garibaldi. Also good: **Toledo/Via Toledo** and **Chiaia** (calmer, prettier, slightly pricier, further from station).
- ⚠️ **Avoid sleeping at:** the blocks immediately around **Piazza Garibaldi / Napoli Centrale and Vasto** (cheap for a reason — sketchy at night), deep **Quartieri Spagnoli** alleys and **Forcella / Rione Sanità** as a *base* (fine to visit daytime). Scampia/Secondigliano irrelevant — far out.

### Options (approx Oct double/night)

|---|---|---|---|
| **B&B Hotel Napoli** | **€50–65** | Right by Garibaldi station (the "acceptable exception" — modern chain building, safe inside) | Reliable chain quality, spotless, A/C, perfect for the 06:00 Circumvesuviana move day. Zero charm, maximum function. [momondo listing](https://www.momondo.com/hotels/naples/B-B-Hotel-Napoli.mhd57343.ksp) |
| **Luna Nuova Suites** | **€60–75** | Centro Storico | Well-reviewed small B&B in the heart of the old town; the "small gap to nicer" pick — modern rooms behind an ancient palazzo facade. [Booking B&B list](https://www.booking.com/bed-and-breakfast/city/it/naples.html) |

**Verdict:** pick Luna Nuova Suites (or any 9.0+ rated Decumani B&B at €60–70) for the experience; B&B Hotel Napoli if you value the station-adjacent convenience for day trips.

## SALERNO — 2 nights (22–24 Oct)

### Where to stay
- **Centro Storico** (around Via dei Mercanti / Duomo) or along **Corso Vittorio Emanuele** between the station and old town — everything is a 5–15 min walk: station, Travelmar ferry dock, restaurants. Salerno is a normal working city, notably calmer than Naples.

### Options (approx Oct double/night)

| **B&B Salerno Centro** | **€50–65** | Center, near Corso | Another converted 1600s convent, modernized, walkable to everything. [bed-and-breakfast.it](https://www.bed-and-breakfast.it/en/campania/salerno-centro-bed-and-breakfast/15810) |

## Booking tactics

1. Book everything on **Booking.com with free cancellation** now-ish (Oct 2026 = 3 months out), then price-check the property's own site (Casa Astarita, Ulisse, Salerno Antica often cheaper direct).
2. Naples & Salerno small B&Bs often collect **city tax in cash** (€2–5/person/night) — keep coins.
3. October is shoulder but **weekends (17–18 Oct = Sat/Sun) price higher** — lock Naples first.
4. Under-budget hack if Sorrento prices spike: base **both** middle nights in Sant'Agnello (Seven Hostel) — identical transport access.
